# Playlitr

#### A modern music streaming platform built for uninterrupted listening, with zero ads and no sign-up required.

---

## Overview

Playlitr is a music app focused on instant, frictionless playback.  
It is designed for users who want to open the app and start listening immediately without being forced into account creation.

The platform emphasizes speed, smooth UI interactions, and a distraction-free experience.

Playlitr is currently in active development, with core playback and performance improvements being expanded.

---

## Why I Built This

I wanted to build a music platform that removes common friction points like mandatory sign-up and ad interruptions.

Most music products focus heavily on monetization-first flows. With Playlitr, the idea was to prioritize user experience first: fast access, clean design, and smooth listening.

This project also helps me explore real-world frontend architecture, caching patterns, and scalable media-centric UI systems.

---

## Features (In Progress)

- Instant music playback without sign-up  
- Ad-free listening experience  
- Clean, minimal interface for discovering and playing songs  
- Smooth transitions and modern interaction design  
- Performance-focused content delivery with caching support  

Some advanced playback and personalization features are under development and will be added in future updates.
